what-is-dms-logchain

dms-logchain is a blockchain solution to log for sign of life. as long as you check in every week, the logchain is updated with a record that indicates you're alive. if you miss a check in, a dead entry is added to the logchain. keep your uids safe and only share with those you want to be able to view your logchain history.
